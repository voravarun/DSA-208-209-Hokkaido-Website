Climate and Clothing

We have some snowy days around November. The city is white with snow in January and February. The months of December through February feature winter festivals. You can see snow falling over the illuminated buildings and roadside trees.It is cold outside, but the houses are warmed inside with heaters. Wearing several layers is recommended. Wearing a thick coat and waterproof boots is necessary.
