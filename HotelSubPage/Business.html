Sapporo Prince Hotel

Just a 3-minute walk from Odori Park and Nishi 11-chome Subway Station, Sapporo Prince Hotel offers rooms with city views and free WiFi. Hot-spring baths and a 28th-floor French restaurant are featured. Guest can benefit from free shuttle from/to JR Sapporo Train Station.

The contemporary air-conditioned rooms at Prince Hotel Sapporo include a satellite flat-screen TV. The attached bathroom has a bathtub. A refrigerator and electric kettle are also provided. more detail...

Royton Sapporo

A 3-minute walk from Nishi 11-Chome Subway Station and a 5-minute drive from JR Sapporo Train Station, Royton Sapporo Hotel offers accommodations with free WiFi and a flat-screen satellite TV.

The spacious rooms feature carpeted floors, a desk and a safety deposit box. Guests can make green tea using the electric kettle and try on nightwear. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and slippers. more detail...

Vessel Hotel Campana Susukino

Conveniently situated in the centre of Sapporo, Vessel Hotel Campana Susukino provides air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private parking. A spa centre and a bicycle rental service are available for guests. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage space for guests.

Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a kettle. At Vessel Hotel Campana Susukino each room comes with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom.more detail...

Sapporo Grand Hotel

Sapporo Grand Hotel boasts a shopping arcade, a gym and a spa centre, a 5-minute walk from JR Sapporo Station via a connecting tunnel. It features 7 dining options, a concierge and free Wi-Fi at the entire property.

Rooms at the Sapporo Grand Hotel are air-conditioned and equipped with a TV and a refrigerator. A work desk, slippers and toiletries are provided.more detail...

Hotel Emisia Sapporo

Conveniently located a 2-minute walk from Shinsapporo Station on the JR and subway lines, Hotel Emisia Sapporo offers free WiFi, rental bicycles and a 24-hour front desk and modern guest rooms. Guests can make a visit to popular tourist attractions such as the Sapporo Clock Tower and Odori Park within a 30-minute train ride.

Sunpiazza Aquarium and the Sapporo Science Centre are a 6-minute walk away, while Mitsui Outlet Park can be accessed within a 15-minute drive. JR Sapporo Train Station is a 13-minute train ride away from the property and New Chitose Airport is a 28-minute ride on the airport express train. more detail...
